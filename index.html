Virtual Lab

Platform for academic research à la digital

  • Overview
  • Documentation

Virtual Lab is a platform for longitudinal surveys and interventions that can be performed entirely within social media platforms such as Facebook Messenger.

Recruit

Use the full power of targeted social media advertising tools to recruit your audience. Adjust parameters as you go to ensure representation across all strata of your target population.

Chat

All interactions with respondents take place within the chat application they already have on their phone, improving their user experience and increasing retention. Send follow up questions after hours, days, weeks or months. Answering your survey is as easy and comfortable as texting a friend.

Randomize

Customize the chat flow by adding rules that use random treatment assignment and/or previous responses, combined in arbitrarily complex ways, to respond to users and send targeted questions.

Intervene

Send images, links, or videos to specific treatment arms. Any time a user clicks on the shared link or plays/pauses/seeks the shared video, their action is recorded for your analysis.

Monitor

Monitor responses in realtime via the Virtual Lab dashboard and download current data in CSV format at any time. See when respondents join, how long they spend, and how many questions they answer.
